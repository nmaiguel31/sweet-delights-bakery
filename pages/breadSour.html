Sourdough Bread

A traditional sourdough loaf with a crisp crust and soft, tangy interior.

Recipe

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 100g active sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, and starter. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Add salt and knead until smooth.
  3. Let the dough ferment for 4-6 hours, folding every hour.
  4. Shape the dough and let it proof overnight in the fridge.
  5. Bake at 230°C (450°F) for 35-40 minutes.
