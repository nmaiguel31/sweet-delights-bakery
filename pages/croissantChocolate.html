Chocolate Croissant

Recipe

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add warm milk and knead into dough.
  2. Roll out dough, layer with butter, and fold several times.
  3. Cut into triangles, add chocolate at the base, and roll into croissants.
  4. Let rise for 1 hour, then brush with egg wash.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
