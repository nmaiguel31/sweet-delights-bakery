Baguette

A classic French bread with a crispy crust and soft interior.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast

Instructions:

  1. Mix yeast with warm water and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture.
  3. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for 1-2 hours until doubled in size.
  5. Shape into baguettes and let them rise again for 30 minutes.
  6. Bake at 450°F (230°C) for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
