Whole Grain Bread

Nutritious and hearty, made with whole grains for a rich flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mix warm water, yeast, and honey. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead for 10 minutes.
  3. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Shape into a loaf and place in a greased pan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes.
