Classic Croissant

Recipe

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)

Instructions

  1. Activate yeast in warm water.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and salt, then add milk and yeast mixture.
  3. Chill the dough, then roll and fold with butter.
  4. Repeat folding and rolling three times.
  5. Cut into triangles, roll into croissants, and let rise.
  6. Brush with egg wash and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes.
