Almond Croissant

A delicious buttery croissant filled with almond cream and topped with sliced almonds and powdered sugar.

Recipe

  • 1 batch of croissant dough
  • 1/2 cup almond paste
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix almond paste, sugar, egg, and almond extract until smooth.
  3. Slice croissants in half and spread the almond filling inside.
  4. Place on a baking sheet and sprinkle sliced almonds on top.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
  6. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.
