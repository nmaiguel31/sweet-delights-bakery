Chocolate Pastry

Rich, decadent, and filled with luscious chocolate layers, our chocolate pastry is a delight for every chocolate lover.

Recipe

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a baking dish and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
