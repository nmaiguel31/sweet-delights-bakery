Classic Bread Recipe

A traditional loaf with a crisp crust and soft interior.

Recipe

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 300ml warm water
  • 10g salt
  • 7g dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
  3. Gradually add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  6. Shape the dough into a loaf and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Let it cool and enjoy!
