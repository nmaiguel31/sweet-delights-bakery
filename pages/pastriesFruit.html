Fruit Tart

A deliciously crisp tart filled with creamy custard and topped with fresh seasonal fruits.

Recipe

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tbsp cold water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruit (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, etc.)
