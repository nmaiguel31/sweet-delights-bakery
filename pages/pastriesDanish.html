Danish Pastry

A flaky, buttery pastry filled with custard, fruit, or almond paste.

Recipe

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (cold)
  • Fruit jam, custard, or almond paste for filling
